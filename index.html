Moody Foodie

Would you rather have a lifetime of regret, or an afternoon of mild disappointment? Either way this is the app for you!
Moody Foodie was created for fans of Bob's Burgers. No, Like seriously, if you're not a fan, this app isnt for you, So SCRAM!
Okay now that they are gone, let me tell you about this app.
Have you ever been watching Bob's Burgers and heard a characters voice and thought "That voice sounds so familiar. Who is that?" Well, you’re not alone.
This app has a random character generator that will go through all the characters and tell you, not only who voiced the actor but when they first appeared on the show.
If you’re more of a burger connoisseur, then this app is still for you. There is a random burger generator that will go through all the “Burger of the Day” options from the show. See a burger that sounds good enough to eat, well problem solved. Just click on the save button to save the recipe for future BBQing.
So, let’s get started, or you could sell your soul. I did and look at me. I’m fine.
